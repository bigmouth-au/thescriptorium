What to expect from writing competitions in 2026

2026 is a strong year for literary competitions, with major prizes across Australia, the UK, USA, Canada and Ireland. Deadlines are spread throughout the year — many of the most prestigious prizes close mid-year, so it’s worth planning ahead.

Entry fees vary widely. Many competitions — particularly those run by writers centres and universities — are free to enter. Others charge a modest reading fee, typically between $10–$30 AUD, which helps fund prize money and operations.

New to entering competitions? Start with free-to-enter prizes to build experience, then work up to the larger competitions. Many winning writers entered the same competitions multiple times before placing.
