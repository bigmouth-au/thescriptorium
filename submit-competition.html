Know one we've missed?

Help us keep the directory comprehensive. Submit a writing competition for review and we'll add verified entries within a few days.

What we list: reputable competitions with clear judging criteria, transparent prize and fee information, and a verifiable track record.
  • We list competitions open to writers in Australia, NZ, UK, Ireland, USA, Canada, or internationally.
  • Vanity contests, pay-to-publish schemes, or competitions with no prize are not listed.
  • All submissions are reviewed before being added. We may edit for clarity and consistency.
