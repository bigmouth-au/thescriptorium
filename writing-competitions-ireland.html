Irish Writing Competitions & Literary Prizes

Ireland punches well above its weight in world literature, and its competition scene reflects that. The Hennessy Literary Awards — one of Ireland's most prestigious prizes for new writing — has launched some of the country's most celebrated literary careers. The Irish Novel Fair connects debut novelists directly with agents and publishers, making it as much an industry opportunity as a competition.

Poetry Ireland runs a wide range of competitions and development opportunities throughout the year, and the Irish Writers Centre supports emerging and established writers with bursaries, mentorships and residencies alongside its competition programme.

Many Irish competitions are open to international writers, particularly those with an Irish connection or writing about Irish themes. Several are open worldwide with no restriction. Always check the eligibility criteria carefully before entering.

Writers based in Australia, the UK, USA or Canada with Irish heritage are often eligible for Ireland-specific awards — it's worth exploring if that applies to you.
